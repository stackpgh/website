Our Amenities

We think hospitality is at the center of the coworking experience.

We try to think of our members as our guests and try to provide them with the best things we can think of.

World-Class Coffee

Our founders love coffee, and we know our members do too. We spend some of our time developing relationships with some of the best roasters in the world so that we can get a rotating selection of some of the best coffee being produced. Our Ratio 8 makes a perfect cup of coffee for one or for a few. We also make sure to have great teas and herbal infusions on hand.

Flexible Workspace

All of our workspace is shared. This gives you the opportunity to work from a standing station, a communal table, a corner chair, a phone booth, or lay down on a couch. We hope that you will find a place at Stack that feels uniquely you.

Escape Pods

We take work seriously. We also know that sometimes you need a break. For the moments when you need a break, you've hit a wall, or you've achieved a victory, we've created a few places to hide out and forget about work. Whether you want to sit and read a book, listen to a record with a friend, watch some of our curated short films, or lose some lives in Megaman, we've got it taken care of.

Technology

We've wrangled up blazing fast internet, a ceiling mounted projection setup, wireless audio, and a few other tech surprises for our members. If there is something that you need, or really really want, to make the space better for you and our other members let us know and we'll try to find a way to make it happen.

About us

Stack is designed to be your home away from home. With a wide variety of workspace options, vaulted ceilings, perfect coffee, and a great community, you will find a place to call your own.

Why We Did This

We've spent years working from home and from coffee shops and decided it was time to put together the best possible work space we could. Once we started we realized that it made sense to share that space with anyone else who wanted to be a part of something new and exciting. We hope that you love this space as much as we do.

Who Can Work at Stack?

Anyone who can do their work on the run but wants a place to settle down can find a home at Stack. We welcome people from all backgrounds, disciplines, and encourage teams to come work here together. Our early members are creatives, social entrepreneurs, designers, programmers, builders, and small business owners. Bring your laptop, your phone, and let us take care of everything else.
